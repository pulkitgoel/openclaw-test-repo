## Example 3: Stock Analysis Notes

Technical Indicators to Remember:
- EMA 5, 50, 100, 200 for trend direction
- EMA 200 crossing = Golden/Death Cross signals
- RSI > 70 = Overbought
- RSI < 30 = Oversold
- Volume spike = Trend change signal

Entry Rules:
- Buy on pullback to support in uptrend
- Avoid buying in strong downtrend without reversal
- Use stop losses below key support levels
- Take partial profits at resistance zones
